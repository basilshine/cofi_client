# Design

## Visual Theme

Warm paper and precise Telegram UI, with dry-pencil details and one confident cobalt field.

## Color

- Paper: `#F4EFE4`
- Light paper: `#FBF8F1`
- Ink: `#171B2D`
- Cobalt: `#2945C7`
- Coral signal: `#FF5A3D`
- Cool shadow: `#DDE2EE`

## Typography

`Golos Text` with a system sans-serif fallback. Large headlines use restrained weight, tight tracking, and short line lengths.

## Shape and Material

Matte paper texture, moderately rounded interactive surfaces, receipt edges, and thin pencil paths. The logo stays small.

## Motion

Messages arrive, a voice waveform moves, and the confirmed expense settles last. Scroll reveals use opacity and transform only. Reduced motion disables choreography.
